How it works

Three steps to a published listing

No copywriting experience needed. The AI does the heavy lifting — you just review and publish.

01
Upload your property photos

Drag and drop up to 10 photos — bedrooms, kitchen, living areas, backyard, exterior. The AI scans every image automatically to understand what it's looking at.

02
Add the basics

Enter bedrooms, bathrooms, suburb, land size, and price. Choose a writing tone — Professional, Luxury, Family, or Investment. That's all we need.

03
Get your content

Receive a full listing description, social media ad copy, and compliance-checked content — all ready to copy directly into your platform of choice.
